About

Below we describe steps towards your perfect trip

  • 1 step:

    You reach us using our contact form.

    Please use this google form to contact us, describe your request, select options, or to arrange a call.

  • 2 step:

    We provide you options, places, and prices

    We have researched and selected options, we are happy to share them with you, to get your opinion and feedback. We make a final list with appointments/places to book and approve it with you.

  • 3 step:

    We arrange all appointments

    We make calls and book all appointments; we make sure that the time is convenient for you, that there are no gaps between your arrangements; nonetheless, we keep a healthy balance, so your day is not heavy and not overloaded.

  • 4 step:

    You pay only 10% for our services

    We send you an official price list from the places you are going to visit, date/times, addresses of your booked appointment. You will pay for your appointments on the spot: e.g., in the medical center or in the hotel.
    You get 24/7 support in case of any questions or unforeseen situations or changes of plans.
    You will pay 10% of the total amount for our services.
    You will receive from us official documents and checks; everything is legal!
